<div id="article"><h1 class="pagetitle">University of North Texas</h1><p class="subtitle">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia.

<p>
The <strong>University of North Texas</strong> (UNT) is located in <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denton%2C_Texas" title="Denton, Texas">Denton</a>, <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">TX</a>, <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States" title="United States">USA</a>.  Formerly the <strong>North Texas State University</strong>, UNT is now the largest university in the north Texas area with more than 20,000 students.

<p>
It is home to the second largest college of <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Music" title="Music">music</a> in the United States, and is particularly renown for its world-class <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jazz" title="Jazz">jazz</a> program.  In addition, the <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Education" title="Education">education</a> program is well-respected.  UNT has just recently started a College of <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Engineering" title="Engineering">Engineering</a> in a separate branch of its campus.  Engineering fields being focused on include Aerospace Engineering, Aeronautical Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Physical Engineering, among others.

<p>
One of the highlights of UNT is the <a class="internal" href="/web/20040310155949/http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Texas_Academy_of_Mathematics_and_Science" title="Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science">Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science</a>, an early college entrance program that annually admits 200 gifted students into full-time college studies following their 10<sup>th</sup> grade year.  This program has produced numerous Intel Talent Search finalists and semi-finalists, Goldwater scholars, and National Merit Scholars.

<p>

<ul><li>University of North Texas - <a class="external" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20040310155949/http://www.unt.edu/" title="http://www.unt.edu/">http://www.unt.edu/</a>
</li><li>Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science - <a class="external" href="https://web.archive.org/web/20040310155949/http://www.tams.unt.edu/" title="http://www.tams.unt.edu/">http://www.tams.unt.edu/</a>
